About Me

I'm Kevin Liu, a mechanical engineer with a passion for exploring the intersections of engineering, technology, and creative expression. My work spans across traditional mechanical engineering disciplines while incorporating modern technological approaches and innovative problem-solving methods.

Currently, I focus on bridging the gap between mechanical engineering principles and emerging technologies. Through my work and personal projects, I explore how traditional engineering practices can be enhanced and reimagined using modern tools and methodologies.

On this site, you'll find:

Essays - Where I share my thoughts on engineering, technology, and their intersection with society. I write about both technical challenges and broader implications of engineering in our world.

Engineering - A showcase of my technical projects and explorations in mechanical engineering. This section documents my hands-on work and practical applications of engineering principles.

Games - A creative outlet where I explore interactive experiences and game development, combining technical skills with creative design.

I believe in sharing knowledge and experiences openly, hoping to contribute to the broader engineering community while continuing to learn and grow in my field.
